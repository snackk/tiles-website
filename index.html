Share Tile

You just took an amazing picture of something you like, or you're just traveling through Europe and maybe you want to meet new people/places

You pick that amazing content you want to share, that could be anything pictures, text, videos,etc. and Tile it!

You can use the privacy setting to define Public/Private/Followers

People can then find your Tile, comment on it, store, share, follow you

Tile's Feed

You've got a friend, or someone who you're following, who Tiled near-by? Perfect!

You got the opportunity to find what that Tile's all about!

All Tiles are geo-location blocked, meaning that you can see who Tiled it, but you can't see it's content, unless you're near it's radius

Take this opportunity to get to known that place you're friend Tiled you from

Store Tile

Haven't got the time right now and Tile is about to expire? Or maybe you just want to keep it

It happens when we're caught up on our daily activities and actually don't have the time to do anything else. We get it!

You can store the Tile. Even after the Tile expired, you can still unlock it's content

Personal Note

Keep track of your order at a restaurant for future meals? Maybe you just want to be reminded of something at that specific spot every time you're near it

Sometime it happens when we want to store some note on a specific spot, for example that meal we want to avoid at a specific restaurant, or our favorite

You can create your personal notes, that are for your eyes only. To remind you whatever you want
